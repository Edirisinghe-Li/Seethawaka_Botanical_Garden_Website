Seethawaka Botanical Garden is a treasure trove of natural beauty and botanical diversity, offering a unique blend of colorful landscapes and peaceful spaces. One of the most enchanting areas is the Rose Garden, featuring over 75 vibrant rose varieties that create a breathtaking display of color and fragrance. Nearby, the Fernery offers a cool, shaded retreat with a stream flowing gently through thousands of native ferns. Visitors are also drawn to the Butterfly Garden, where delicate butterflies flutter among flowering plants, and the Orchard, which showcases a variety of fruit-bearing trees. For those interested in Sri Lanka’s traditional plant knowledge, the Medicinal and Spice Gardens provide a rich learning experience on the island’s herbal heritage.
Nature lovers can take in panoramic views from the Viewpoint, overlooking the lush garden and the distant Sabaragamuwa mountain range—a perfect photo opportunity. The garden’s Lake and Ponds add a serene atmosphere, enhanced by lotus flowers and aquatic life. Visitors can unwind in the elegant Japanese Garden, stroll through the peaceful Pebble Garden, or rest in the shaded Kumbuk Grove, surrounded by towering native trees. Whether you're exploring exotic flora, enjoying the scenery, or simply sitting quietly by the lake, every corner of the garden is designed to offer a sense of calm, beauty, and connection to nature.

Nature’s Masterpieces:
What to See at Seethawaka Garden

Places

Rose Garden

A vibrant collection of over 75 different rose varieties, displaying a spectrum of colors from red velvet to yellow, orange, pink, and purple.

Places

Fernery

A shaded area with a cool stream running through it, providing the perfect climate for the growth of over a thousand fern species native to the nation's wet zone.

Places

Medicinal and Spice Gardens

Dedicated sections showcasing a variety of medicinal plants and spices, reflecting Sri Lanka's rich herbal heritage.

Places

Butterfly Garden

A habitat designed to attract and support various butterfly species, providing visitors with the opportunity to observe these colorful insects up close.

Places

Orchid Garden

A vibrant display of rare and exotic orchids, showcasing delicate blooms in a variety of colors and forms, perfect for flower lovers and photographers.

Places

Pebble Garden

An aesthetically pleasing arrangement of pebbles and stones, adding to the garden's diverse landscapes.

Places

Lake and Ponds

A flower-decked lake complemented by a series of ponds, enhancing the garden's tranquil atmosphere.

Places

Japanese Garden

A meticulously landscaped area featuring traditional Japanese design elements, including a small bridge and sand garden, offering a serene and picturesque setting.

Places

Kumbuk Grove

A distinctive area within the garden featuring large Kumbuk trees, offering a cool, shaded spot for relaxation.
